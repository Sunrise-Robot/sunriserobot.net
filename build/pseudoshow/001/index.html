#001 - April 8, 2014

Too Many Detectives

Justin and Michael revive the long-dormant Pseudobook Podcast with special guests Adam McCabe, Henry Kaiser, and Matthew Clay - the stars of Detective Detective Detective. They discuss production of the film, living cramped in a cabin, and the kind of creative community they'd like to be part of. Not actually sponsored by Cazt.
